On The Route With Trustaurant: Find Trusted Restaurants Along Your Route

Finding a suitable restaurant to eat while travelling can be difficult. Most of the time, people end up eating at unfamiliar places that do not match their preferences. Deciding what to eat and where to eat can become even more challenging while travelling across long distances, whether you are alone, with friends, with your family or perhaps you are visiting a new place. Usually, the very first thing people do is search for restaurants near them and settle for the nearest option or a restaurant that looks slightly decent.

So, what if there was a dining app that could exactly show you the list of reliable restaurants that reside along your travel route? To address this challenge, Trustaurant introduced its On the Way feature. On the Way is a route-based restaurant discovery feature that helps users identify verified restaurants located along a selected travel route, rather than random nearby suggestions. It is specifically designed for travellers, families and anyone seeking a comfortable and trusted dining experience while on the move. Unlike conventional dining apps, Trustaurant does not randomly suggest restaurants. In fact, every restaurant partnered with our platform is verified based on strict eligibility criteria. This allows our users to plan their dining more seamlessly without worrying about quality, hygiene or unnecessary detours from the travel route.

The regular dining or table reservation apps are built somewhat differently as they largely focus on location proximity or availability. On the other hand, Trustaurant focuses on the travel context of dining, by curating a more complete and family-friendly dining experience. We focus on giving you a clear view of the location, ambience and currently available food menus of the restaurant even before you arrive. We understand the importance of parking facilities, clean and accessible washrooms and updated menus especially for families and travellers. At Trustaurant we know that waiting unnecessarily isn’t ideal while travelling, and thus we do our best to reduce this inconvenience. The On the Way feature is designed to simplify the process of discovering suitable restaurants during a journey, exactly as the name suggests.

HOW TO USE THE ON THE WAY FEATURE?

This feature is designed to be user-friendly and simple. To use it, you just have to open the Trustaurant app and follow these steps:

Select your Route

Enter your starting location and your destination. If your device location is enabled, the app can automatically track your starting point. This could be a short travel or a long highway journey across Kerala.

Discover restaurants along your Route

Once the route is mapped, Trustaurant will show you the list of restaurants that are located along or close to the selected route. These restaurants are verified partners available on the Trustaurant platform.

Compare Multiple Routes

When there are multiple available routes for the same destination, you can select your preferred route, and Trustaurant shows you dining places associated with the selected route, helping you make a more informed decision.

WHAT ON THE WAY DOES NOT DO

The On the Way feature is designed to assist with dining discovery during travel, but it is important to understand its scope and limitations. On the Way does not provide turn-by-turn navigation or real-time traffic guidance. It does not account for temporary road closures, traffic congestion or live driving conditions. Restaurants shown through this feature are limited to verified Trustaurant partner restaurants and may not include all dining options available along a route. While restaurants are selected based on proximity to the travel route, minor deviations may still be required depending on road access and entry points. The feature currently operates within supported regions and availability may vary based on location.
