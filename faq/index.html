Everything you need to know about dining with confidence

Trustaurant is a dining and reservation app that helps you discover, plan, and enjoy your dining experience hassle free. Key features include:

  • Finding restaurants nearby and along travel routes
  • Viewing live restaurant menus
  • Checking ambience through images and 360° views
  • Reserving tables and ordering food in advance

Trustaurant can be used by anyone looking for a better dining experience. It is ideal for food enthusiasts, families, travellers and anyone looking for the best restaurants near them to choose from.

Trustaurant partners only with verified restaurants that are carefully reviewed before being listed on the platform for a comfortable dining experience. These restaurants are selected on the basis of:

  • Availability of parking facilities
  • Spacious dining areas
  • Separate and clean washroom facilities
  • Family-friendly spaces

This ensures you discover only quality dining options when searching for restaurants near you.
