How We Choose Our Partner Restaurants

At Trustaurant, we're committed to providing our customers with the best dining experiences. To ensure this, our team thoroughly verifies every potential partner restaurant before adding them to our network. We consider several important factors to guarantee that only the highest-quality, most reliable restaurants make the cut.

Our selection process includes:

  • Parking Facilities – We assess whether the restaurant offers ample, convenient parking for families and travelers.
  • Dining Space – We ensure that the dining area is spacious, clean, and comfortable enough for a relaxed meal with family or friends.
  • Ambiance & Cleanliness – A welcoming atmosphere and stringent hygiene standards are non-negotiable for our team.
  • Separate Washroom Facilities – We prioritize restaurants that offer separate washroom facilities, making sure the space is family-friendly.

Each restaurant is carefully inspected, and only those that meet our high standards are chosen to be a part of the Trustaurant network. When you dine with us, you can rest assured that you're in a trusted, quality space.
